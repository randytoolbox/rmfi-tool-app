THE ALGORITHM

How the robot picks.

📡

Score every stock

Each morning the robot pulls 14 months of price history for 440+ S&P 500 stocks. It scores each one on distance from 52-week high, daily momentum, volume surge, and strength vs. SPY. Max score: 100 pts.

🛡

Filter the noise

RSI gates keep the robot out of overbought momentum chases (>70) and free-falling knives (<35). If earnings are within 7 days, it waits. If the stock is below its 200-day moving average, it skips.

⚡

Buy & monitor

Top 3 scores get ~$20,000 each as notional market orders via Alpaca. Every afternoon it checks P&L — stop loss at −5%, target at +5%, max hold 5 trading days.
